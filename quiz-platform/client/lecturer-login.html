What you can do here

The lecturer portal supports a full workflow from quiz creation to session monitoring and results.

  • Create quizzes manually or with AI assistance Generate questions, review them, and save them into the platform.
  • Launch sessions in 3 game modes Classic, Battle Royale, and Tactical Duel are available from the same lecturer workflow.
  • Monitor live student activity Use the dedicated monitoring page to follow active sessions without mixing it into the lobby.
  • Review end-of-session results See final scores and session summaries after the game ends.
