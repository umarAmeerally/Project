Final Year Project

Gamified Quiz Platform

A web-based quiz platform that supports Classic mode, Battle Royale, Tactical Duel, and AI-assisted question generation for lecturers.

Platform Entry

Choose your role

Lecturers manage quizzes and live sessions. Students join using a session code and participate in the active game mode.

  • Lecturer Create quizzes, generate AI-assisted questions, start sessions, monitor gameplay, and review final results.
  • Student Join a live session, wait for the lecturer to launch the game, play the quiz mode, and view your final result.
