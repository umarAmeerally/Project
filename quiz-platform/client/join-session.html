Join a Session

Enter the session code given by your lecturer and choose a nickname to join the live game.

Before You Join

What happens next

Once you join, you will wait in the session room until the lecturer starts the game.

  • Classic Mode You answer the quiz normally and receive your result at the end.
  • Battle Royale You compete across elimination rounds until one winner remains.
  • Tactical Duel A focused 2-player tactical match with actions, HP, and timed decisions.
  • Important Your nickname must be unique within the session.
